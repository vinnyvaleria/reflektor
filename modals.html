The game consists of 2 grids :
Main (left) and Secondary (right)

Your goal is to move both characters on the Main grid and Secondary grid each to destination X.

  1. You will only be able to control the character in Main grid.
  2. Your other character in the Secondary grid will move in a mirrored direction.
  3. Use the arrow buttons on the screen or on your keyboard to move the characters.
  4. If you hit any of the obstacles (brick wall, grass or big tree), it will be GAME OVER!
  5. Have fun and be on the leaderboard!
